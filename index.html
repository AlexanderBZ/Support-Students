Support Students

The COVID-19 Pandemic has caused a complete change in life as we know it. With this transition, many students have undergone an increase in stress and anxiety. Support Students' goal is to help relieve some of this stress through providing free resources via. our website and newsletter.

Resources

There are a number of great resources below for students to manage stress and wellness during these pandemic times. We've curated the best examples here in our resources page.

Podcasts

Podcasts recently have exploded in popularity. If you're a regular podcast listener you might want to try some of our highlighted podcasts for maintaining mental health.

Help Lines

Have you felt anxious recently about the changes happening in not only your life, but the whole world? You might want to consider talking about it with a qualified Crisis Counselor via the Help Line provided on our resources page.

Meditation

Meditation has been proven to help calm and destress people. Why not try some of our recommended free meditation lessons?
